Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2, 2025

1. Introduction

FileToQR (hereinafter 'we', 'us', or 'our') takes your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and protected when you use our website or services.

2. Information Collection

Our core design principle is 'privacy first'. Most features process your files and data locally within your browser and do not transmit them to our servers. However, the following information may be collected:

  • Usage Data: How you use our services (pages visited, features used, etc.)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • IP Address: For security monitoring and regional service optimization purposes

3. Local Processing

When converting files or generating QR codes, all processing is done locally in your browser. Unless you explicitly use a feature that requires cloud processing (which will be clearly indicated), your files and QR code content are not sent to our servers.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We may use cookies and similar technologies to improve our services and analyze usage patterns. Examples:

  • Essential Cookies: Provide basic website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Understand service usage (using anonymized data)
  • Advertising Cookies: Deliver personalized ads

You can manage cookie settings in your browser.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services for analytics, advertising, and feature provision. These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies. Currently used third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: Website usage analysis
  • Google AdSense: Advertising delivery

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child has provided us with information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own, where privacy laws may differ. By using our services, you consent to such transfers.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your country of residence, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access: Request access to your information
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your information
  • Right to Object to Processing: Object to the processing of your information
  • Right to Data Portability: Request the transfer of your information

Please contact us at the address below to exercise your rights.

10. Policy Changes

This Privacy Policy may be changed from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting them on this page and updating the 'Last updated' date.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@filetoqr.com
